Our Story

A small room, built with a big idea.

Aveno Café & Events began with a simple observation: the most meaningful celebrations are often the smallest ones — a birthday with eight close friends, an evening watching the match together, a quiet proposal. Yet there was nowhere private, stylish, and affordable to host them.

So we built one. Rooted in Chandipur, Purba Medinapur, Aveno is a private hall designed entirely around small-group celebrations — decorated to theme, lit to mood, and reserved exclusively for you and your guests, hour by hour.
